A quick browser game which you defeat your enemies with tactics, luck and skill.

Choose your Character!

Warrior

Warriors have high health and can deal large amounts of damage. They are slower and don't use that many skills however.

Rogue

Rogues are fast and have better chances to land critical hits. They lack health and strength.

Mage

Mages can deal massive amounts of damage and has extra utility with their spells. This does mean they rely on their mana and slack on health and strength.

Hunter

Jack of all trades. Well rounded with good range abilities to use, but don't really excel in anything.

Knight

Knights have excellent health and decent strength backed up by holy magic. They are cautious thusly slower and lack the skill to land critical hits often.
